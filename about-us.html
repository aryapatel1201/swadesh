About Us

We SWADESH CONSUMER INC. Are BIS & ISO 9001: 2000 certified organization engaged in manufacturing & exporting a wide range of Disinfectant and Antiseptic Raw Material, API (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients) and Emulsifier, These are highly effective, safe& consistent products.

We are manufacturers, Exporters, and Suppliers of Finished Pharmaceutical products like Antiseptic and Disinfectant Chemical, Ointment Cream. We SWADESH CONSUMER INC. has our venture established in Gujarat, India. under the name  SWADESH CONSUMER INC. Our core competence is to cater to the ever-changing and evolving requirements of our quality-conscious clients who are looking for manufacturers committed to quality along with timely delivery and competitive prices.

We SWADESH CONSUMER INC. always believes in fulfilling the social responsibilities, maintaining the extending support to the society apart from the business motives. Our R&D division constantly strives to improve existing processes (within the regulatory framework) for cost reduction, develop new intermediates, and perform custom synthesis as per customer's requirements. One of the best pharmaceutical manufacturing companies in India,  SWADESH CONSUMER INC. has been approved with BIS & ISO 9001: 2000 certifications that meet all quality parameters and norms
